# {{title}}

## Decision

State the actual decision in one or two sentences, as plainly as possible.

## Context

What situation or problem prompted this decision. What was true at the time
that made a choice necessary.

## Options Considered

- **Option A** — brief description, pros/cons
- **Option B** — brief description, pros/cons
- **Option C** — brief description, pros/cons

## Reasoning

Why the chosen option won — the actual reasoning, not just the conclusion.
This section is the most valuable part of a decision note; it's what future
you (or an agent) needs when re-evaluating whether the decision still holds.

- [[Person B]]

## Consequences / Follow-Ups

What this decision commits you to, and what needs to happen as a result.

## Revisit Conditions

What would make this decision worth revisiting — a specific trigger or date,
not just "if things change." If this decision is later reversed or
superseded, archive this note per `conventions/editing-rules.md` rather than
editing it in place to reflect the new decision.
